Questions

Good to know

Who is Execulist for?

Anyone who runs their day on calls, quotes, visits, and errands — contractors, agents, sales reps, solo operators. If your day doesn't fit a flat list, it fits here.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Your board lives on your device, so it works with or without a signal, and reminders fire locally.

Can I cancel the trial?

Anytime, from your Google Play subscriptions. Cancel before day three and you're never charged.

Where's my data stored?

On your phone. Execulist has no account and no server collecting your tasks. Uninstalling clears it.
